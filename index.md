## A quick overview  
* Free web based app providing information about US Storm Events
* Based on [NOAA's](http://www.noaa.gov/) Storm database
* Quick and easy analysis of Storm Events across the US.
* Sorting by:
  + Event Type
  + Fatalities/Property Damage
  + Year
  + .. State
